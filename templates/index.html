About

Style EQ is a neural-network based style transfer system. It was designed and built by Chris Kedzie and Katy Gero.

How it works: Given an input sentence, it first finds a "pivot" sentence with similar length and parts-of-speech that is in the desired style. The idea is to make the input sentence more syntacticaly similar to the pivot sentence. The neural network then takes the input sentence stripped of its syntactic features (punctuation, prepositions, etc.) and tries to rewrite the sentence with the syntactic feautres of the pivot sentence.

StyleEQ is based on research published on our paper Low-Level Linguistic Controls for Style Transfer and Content Preservation.
